Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All plans are cancel-anytime with no penalty. Your subscription stays active until the end of the billing period — mapped to the cancelled_at and ends_at fields in our system.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN & Airtel Uganda), Visa/Mastercard, and bank transfers for enterprise plans.

What happens when my trial ends?

If you signed up with a free trial (trial_ends_at), you'll receive a 3-day reminder. You can upgrade or downgrade to Free with no charge.

Is yearly billing worth it?

Yearly plans save 20% compared to paying monthly — billed as a single annual payment. The savings are calculated automatically using the yearly_savings attribute.
