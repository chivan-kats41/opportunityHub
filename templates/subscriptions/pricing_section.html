Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All plans are cancel-anytime with no penalty. Your subscription stays active until the end of the billing period.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN & Airtel Uganda), Visa/Mastercard, and bank transfers for enterprise plans.

What happens when my trial ends?

You'll receive a 3-day reminder. You can upgrade or downgrade to the Free plan with no charge.

Is yearly billing worth it?

Yearly plans save 20% compared to paying monthly — billed as a single annual payment.
